Liver Longer

We offers the best system to match liver with recipients and to predict the success rate of liver transplantation surgery.
You can live longer!

Matching

Approximately 14,000 patients are listed and waiting for orthotopic liver transplantation(OLT) but only 7,000 OLTs are performed annually. This model can find the best match between a given liver and a list of recipients to save more people and more resource.

Prediction

The ability to predict graft failure or primary nonfunction at liver transplant decision time assists utilization of scarce resource of donor livers, while ensuring that patients who are urgently requiring a liver transplant are prioritized.

Project Information

Liver transplant is a lifesaving procedure for many liver-damaging conditions: End stage cirrhosis, liver failure, or a cancerous lesion of the liver.
This project was initialized to reduce futility in liver transplantation and to increase the life-span and life quality of patients.
